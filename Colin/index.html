Projects

Measuring Temperature and Humidity with Arduino

In this project I use an Arduino and a DHT11 Sensor to measure the environments Temperature and Humidity. That data goes through a formula to find the heat index. Heat index is what it feel like outside, it is the same temperature that a weather channel or website will report to you. It then displays this information on a lcd wired to the arduino.
